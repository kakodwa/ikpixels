Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We work with clients worldwide. We're experienced in remote collaboration and use modern tools to ensure smooth communication across different time zones.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, PayPal, and local mobile money services (Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba). Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, and maintenance. We also provide training to help you manage your new website or application.

Can you help with existing projects or only new ones?

We can help with both! Whether you need to improve an existing website, add new features, fix bugs, or start from scratch, our team is ready to assist.
